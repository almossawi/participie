Decide how the US budget should be spent by resizing the pie's slices using the sliders. A slider always modifies the slice to its left. Reduce a slice first so that you can increase another one.

Roll over a part of the pie to see the budget function that it represents.

When you're done, submit your pie. We'll collect everyone's pies and then consolidate them into something meaningful for you as well as the 2012 presidential candidates!
